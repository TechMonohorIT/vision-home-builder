In the evolving suburb of Melonba, this bespoke home is a standout example of contemporary suburban luxury. Featuring a bold mix of concrete render and matte finishes, the facade offers instant street appeal. Internally, the home is equally striking, with soaring ceilings and an open-concept layout that promotes natural flow.

Every space is finished with premium textures, from oak timber floors to custom stonework in the bathrooms. The kitchen features dual ovens, a walk-in pantry, and pendant lighting that elevates the entire room. With landscaped surrounds and a peaceful neighbourhood setting, this property is both modern and timeless.
